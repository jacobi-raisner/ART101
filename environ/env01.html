You are lost in an empty basketball arena miles away from civilization. All you remember is a strange whisper coming in your dreams the previous night...

Whatever door you choose to take will determine your fate...

In your thoughts you hear whispers again...

    hello?
The voice comes from a basketball that sits beside you. It rolled a long ways to get there you think, but aren't sure.

You pick it up slowly. You notice it shouldn't feel like it does. Why does it feel squishy?

    If you take a shot from the three point line and make it you will win....

    one million...

    basketballs

You think for a moment about the proposition but then realise...

you don't want one million basketballs.

    What the ball does next no one saw coming. Should you have accepted it's offer?
